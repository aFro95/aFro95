ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm George! I'm a Student & Noobie Developler!

I am currently studying the Master's program "Theory of Coding and Information Storage" at the Faculty of Applied Sciences, National University of Science and Technology Politehnica Bucharest. I chose this master's degree to develop my knowledge and skills in cyber security and defensive security. My desire is to work in the field and contribute to protecting information and systems against threats growing cyber threats.

Regarding my skills, I would like to mention that I actively participated in developing my IT skills by completing two courses. The first of these was a Python programming course ("100 days of code: The Complete Python Pro Bootcamp for 2023") on Udemy website, where I learned from the basics of software development in this programming language to the creation of medium-advanced level projects.

"The Complete 2023 Web Development Bootcamp" on the same site was the second course I completed, which covered a wide range of topics such as HTML, CSS, Javascript.

I would also like to mention that I also gained knowledge on the Computer Hardware side, although I don't have an official certification in this regard. My passion for exploring and understanding the technical details of hardware components such as processors, motherboards, video cards, etc. has materialized over the years through troubleshooting various problems with family computers, groups of friends and my own pc. In addition, I have successfully created systems from scratch, carefully selecting compatible components and ensuring that systems work efficiently and smoothly.
